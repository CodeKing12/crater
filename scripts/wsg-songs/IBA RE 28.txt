IBA RE

iba re

alabe inu asa si
(The great protector)

Emi mi toti selogo
(My spirit that you have honored)

Tun pada wa dupe
(is here again to give you thanks)

Ese Ese
(Thank you)

Ese Ese
(Thank you)

Emi mi toti selore
(My spirit that you've honored)

Tun pada wa dupe
(Is here again to give you thanks)

Iba ree oluda aye
(I pay homage to the creator of the universe)

Oba to jujuju bie ni baro
(The greatest one)

Oba mimimi emi inu omo eniyan
(The living breath we breathe)

Paripari ola,oba tin gbadobale oba
(Majesty, king of all kings)

Egbe leyin asotito
(Faithful one)

Kade pe lori
(Your royal highness)

Kibata pe lese,ewo ase lenu oba
(Majesty full of authority)

Emi sun moba ni won ebe kan
(I adore you)

Mo jinasoba ni won ebe mefa
(I honor you)

Oba mi ajiki
(My king worthy of praise)

Oba mi ajike
(My king worthy of honor)

Oun goro lale biiro ara nla
(Mightier than any thunder)

Afefe tin fe,ile a si ma mi titi
(Very great indeed you are)

Eledumare to raye rorun
(The one who sees heaven and Earth)

Eletente sebi egborun kan
(Mighty one in heaven)

Eletente,e da yo laarin irawo
(You are more beautiful than all heavenly bodies)

Baba mi ogbamugbamu oju orun o se gbamu
(My Father, unstoppable God)

Okele gbungbu to soro gbemi
Oba to moye irawo
(You know the number of all the stars)

Jagunlabi loko mi
(My husband)

Jagunlabi lore mi
(My friend)

Ogbagba tin gba lai lara
(Saviour)

Ogbeni nija ma fi tibon se ni
(Supporter)

Enikerin ninu ina
(The fourth person in the fire)

Sebi iwo lojo,owo lara,iwo nina
Iwo lo mi
(You are so great beyond any description)

Eru tin deru beru nii
(even fear is afraid of you)

Ese Ese
(Thank you)

Ese Ese
(Thank you)

Emi mi toti selore
(My spirit that you've honored)

Tun pada wa dupe
(Is here again to give you thanks)

Mo loba olola
(I have a king full of honor)

Lade lade lon faso rite re ka
(The elders casts their crowns before your throne)

Moni Teru tomo lon
(Everyone worships you)

Awon ara word sanctuary lasuretete fogo foba alade
(Word sanctuary honors you)

Akomo jajo
(The one who sends on an errand)

abani joruko koju matomo
(Yet, he helps to carry out the same, so I won't be ashamed)

Emi funra mi pe ni oba abanija majebi
(I call you the king who is never found faulty)

Obami atogbojule
(My king, my confidant)

Obami komboki oro
(My king, the depth of words)

Obami agbalagba, kikioye
(My king, the ancient one, Majesty......)
