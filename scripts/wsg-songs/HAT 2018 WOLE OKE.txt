Wole Oke is a graduate of Architecture at the University of Lagos, Nigeria in 1991. He is based in Lagos, Nigeria and currently the Managing

Partner of Azimuth Nigeria Limited, a design and construction company established in 1991.

He has been in practice for 27 years, working locally and internationally

He gave his life to Christ in 1994 and has never regretted that decision, growing into a devout minister of the gospel.

He has been involved in the set-up and construction of various worship centres within Lagos.

He is happily married and blessed with a wonderful daughter.
