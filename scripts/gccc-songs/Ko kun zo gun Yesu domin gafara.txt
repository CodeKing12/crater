Ko kun zo gun Yesu domin gafara
(will you come to Jesus for forgiveness)

(He is calling us all to come)
Shi ya ba da ran sa domin dukan mu
( He's the one who gave his life for us all)
Sai ku zo wurin Yesu, ku zo
( So come to Jesus, ye come)

(He's the one who sanctifies from sin)
Sai ku zo wurin Yesu, ku zo
(So come,to Jesus, ye come)

Kome yawan laifi fa Zai wanke ku

(So come,to Jesus, ye come)
