Verse 1
God, give us Christian home!
Home where the Bible is loved and taught,
Home where the Master's will is sought,

Home crowned with beauty Your love has wrought;
God, give us Christian home;
God give us Christian home.

Verse 2
God, give us Christian home!
Home where the father is true and strong,
Home that is free from the blight wrong,

Home that are joyous with love and song;
God, give us Christian home;
God give us Christian home.

Verse 3
God, give us Christian home!
Home where the mother, in caring quest,
Strive to show others Your way is the best,

Home where the lord is an honoured guest,
God, give us Christian home;
God give us Christian home.

Verse 4
God, give us Christian home!
Home where the children are led to know,
Christ in His beauty who loves them so,

Home where the altar's fire burn and glow;
God, give us Christian home;
God give us Christian home.
