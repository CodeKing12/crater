oro olorun!

Bawo ni mose lese afihan ope bi oro olorunse yi aye mi pada. (How can I describe the precepts of how the Word Of God changed me)

Mo ronu igbakan seyin ti mo sonu sinu aye ese yi..
(There was that time that I was lost to this World of Sin and Death)

ese aye  mumi bi omi okun... Mo was n rapala bi edun arinle. (Sin once filled me till I was drown in it)

Sugbon oro olorun to mi wa pelu agbara lati yi ayemi pada.( But The Word came to me to change my life forever)

oro iye ni oro naa, oro ologo julo sini, Oro yi lagbara.. osi mu ju idakida oloju meji ti ngunni lo....( This Very Word is The Word of Life..most glorious.. sharper than any two edged sword)

Oro yi lo gun okan ati emi mi , titi de orike rike ati ora inu egungun mi.. ( He pierced my person dividing away the unnecessary)

Oro to mo ero inu ati ete okan enikeni sini pelu..
(He knows the thoughts and intent of my heart)

oro olorun ni ifihan olorun simi,  (God's word is the revelation of himself to me..)

nitori oro re ni imole to to lati  fi oye fun awon ope.
( In This Word, The Called have the Light which gives understanding )

oro yi ni imisi olorun to komi ni ibawi, o tunmise, o tomi si ona ododo re, osi fun mi ni gbogbo ohun to t fun ise rere na..( This is the very Proficient Word for Correction and raising for Ministry )

oro re pami mo lowo ese, idanwo eran ara ati iku, osi fun mi ni ore ofe lati so mi di omo bibi re ( His Word Keeps and gives Grace that we might be Children of God)

Olorun mi ...emi  ko ni ayan miran ju di diran mo oro re.. (My Lord.. I have no doubt that your Word is worth my Living Time)

nitori oro re dun mo mi lenu ju oyin lo,  ( For your Word is sweeter than honey in my mouth)

oro re si ni fitila fun ese mi ati imole si ipa ona mi ( He is a lamp unto my feet and a Light unto my path)

Ki wa lode timi mi o  ni diran mo oro naa ni ojojumo (So why won't I be yours Everytime).

oro re lo ti wa lati atetekose, oro yi  wa pelu olorun, olorun sini oro na (Your Word has been from the Start with God..and God is the Word)

oro re lo pinleshe ohun gbogbo toun be... (He is the Source of all that's in existence)

mosi mo wipe oro re lo nimi, ohun lo dami..Oro re si lo momi totun  momi, (Your Word owns my entirety)

Olorun mi..moti she ipinnu ninu okan mi pe kosi ohun to le ya mi kuro ninu ife re.. eyi ti se  oro  re..(Lord, my life is pledged to you.. yes your Word)

Nitori ti olorun feranmi, ofun mi ni oro re, mo gba oro naa osi ra emi mi pada. (For God loved and gave me his Word..I believed..and he Changed me)

lowo iparun ayeraye
Lowo iku ati ipo oku ( He saved me from eternal destruction)

oro re komi lati gbe igbeaye mimo ati lati je omo olorun
osi fun mi ni agbara, (He taught me How to live as Sons indeed..He empowered me)

osomi di oba ati woli pelu .(Then I became a King and A Priest)

ogbemi si ori oke re, o fumi ni aso titun osi demi lade ogo re. (He set me on his hill.. changed my garment and crowned me with Glory)

Egbo na, ta wa ni oro iye to to se ohun nla yi (Who then is this Word of Life)

JESU KRISTI ti NASAReTI.. ohun na ni oRo ..
ohun loku,  totun jinde nitori temi ati ire(He is Jesus Christ of Nazareth..who died but rose again for me and you)

owa lati fi oro han .....eyi ti se arare ( Yes, he revealed Himself)

Olorunmi...orun ati aye akoja lo... sugbon oro re asi ma wa titi lai lai (My Lord.. Heaven and Earth may be no more but you word stands sure)

Kiki awon Toba si n tele ki yo see alaini oun to Dara...
( And as Many as Follow him shall lack nothing good)

AMIN  (Amen)
