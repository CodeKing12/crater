# Working with Scripture

Crater makes it easy to display Bible verses during your service. This guide covers everything you need to know about finding and displaying scripture.

## Accessing Scripture

1. Click on the **Scripture** tab at the bottom of the control window
2. You'll see the scripture browser panel

## Choosing a Bible Translation

Crater comes with multiple Bible translations. To switch between them:

1. In the Scripture tab, look for the translation selector
2. Click on it to see available translations (e.g., NKJV, KJV, NIV)
3. Select your preferred translation
4. The scripture list will update to show that translation

## Browsing Scripture

### Method 1: Manual Browsing

1. The scripture list shows all available verses
2. Scroll through to find the passage you need
3. Verses are organized by book, chapter, and verse

### Method 2: Quick Search (Recommended)

The quick search feature lets you jump directly to any verse using a simple format:

**Format:** `Book Chapter:Verse`

**Examples:**
- `John 3:16` - Goes to John chapter 3, verse 16
- `Gen 1:1` - Goes to Genesis chapter 1, verse 1
- `Ps 23:1` - Goes to Psalm 23, verse 1

**To use quick search:**

1. Click the search icon to switch to quick search mode (tree icon)
2. Type the reference (e.g., "John 3:16")
3. Press Enter or click the result
4. The verse will be highlighted

### Method 3: Text Search

Search for specific words within the Bible:

1. Click the search icon to switch to search mode (magnifying glass icon)
2. Type the word or phrase you're looking for
3. Results matching your search will appear
4. Click on a result to select it

## Selecting Verses

Once you've found your scripture:

1. **Single-click** a verse to preview it (see it in the Preview Panel)
2. **Double-click** a verse to send it live (display on projection)

## Displaying Multiple Verses

When you select a verse, Crater shows:
- The book and chapter reference
- The verse number
- The verse text

To display consecutive verses:

1. Double-click the first verse to go live
2. Use the **Arrow Down** key to move to the next verse
3. Use the **Arrow Up** key to go to the previous verse

## Scripture Reference Display

When scripture is displayed, the audience sees:
- The verse text (large and readable)
- The reference (book, chapter, verse)
- The translation abbreviation

The exact layout depends on your selected scripture theme.

## Tips for Scripture Display

### Prepare Before Service

1. Look up your passages before the service starts
2. Add them to your schedule in order
3. Test that each passage displays correctly

### During Service

1. Stay one verse ahead - preview the next verse
2. Watch the speaker for cues to advance
3. Use keyboard navigation for quick changes

## Adding Scripture to Schedule

To add a scripture passage to your service schedule:

1. Find the verse in the Scripture tab
2. Right-click on the verse
3. Select "Add to Schedule"
4. The verse will appear in your Schedule Panel

## Common Tasks

### Display a single verse
1. Use quick search: `John 3:16`
2. Double-click to go live

### Display a passage (multiple verses)
1. Find the starting verse
2. Add it to the schedule
3. Navigate through verses using arrow keys during service

### Switch between translations
1. Use the translation selector
2. Choose a different version
3. Find the same passage again
