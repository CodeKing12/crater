The Anointing

There is only one being in this world
Born by God's word
Stronger than fire
The only being that can
Lift mortal man higher
Making him immortal

He is the force that
Transforms everything HE touches
Making it change forever
We gain this being by faith,
Knowledge of the word of God,
By the communion with the Holy Ghost.
He whom is touched by this being is changed
He who has this being
Can change anything
The being is none other than THE ANOINTED ONE AND HIS ANOINTING
