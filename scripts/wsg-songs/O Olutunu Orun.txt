O OLUTUNU ORUN

Verse 1

Si o Olutunu Orun
(To thee, O Comforter divine,)

Fun ore at
( for all thy grace and power benign,)

A nko, Aleluya
(sing we alleluia! alleluia!)


Verse 2

Si O, ife eni t
( To thee, whose faithful love had place)

Ninu Majemu Olorun
(in God's great covenant of grace,)

A nko, Aleluya
(sing we alleluia! alleluia!)


Verse 3

Si O agbara Eni ti
( To thee, whose faithful power doth heal,)

O nwe ni mo, t
( enlighten, sanctify, and seal,)

A nko, Aleluya
(sing we alleluia! alleluia!)

Verse 5

Si O, Eniti Kristi ran
